Rinvoq has now been used in approved indication for active psoriatic arthritis.

Improvement in ACR20 rates with RINVOQ 15mg was reported as early as week 2. At week 24, 73% of patient treated with RINVOQ 15mg achieved ACR 20 compared with 67% and 45% for those treated with adalimumab and placebo. The efficacy of Rinvoq 15mg maintains to week 104.
